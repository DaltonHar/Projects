Why I Code

So, you really wanna know why I started coding? Well, I think it's just because.
I honestly don't know why exactly I wanted to start. A list of reasons I can think of that might be why are:

  1. My friends are doing it
  2. Further and/or change my career
  3. Improve myself
  4. It's something different
But I will know for sure why I'm coding in the near future. I don't know exactly where it will take me in the near future, but part of me is hoping to change my career and veer away from jobs that require direct contact with customers.

My Favorite Thing about Learning Coding

My favorite thing thus far would definitely be seeing my improvement overall after only a week of learning HTML and CSS. Creating this website by myself I know will be my greatest achievement and one of the biggest steps I have taken in learning coding, since I began.
